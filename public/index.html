trntxt.uk

Welcome to Train Text: a data-friendly UK train times site.



Usage

Either use the boxes above or add to this page's URL to search for stations. Station names can be entered using either their 3 letter station codes, or their name e.g. 'bristolparkway', 'bristolp' or 'bpw' will get you times for Bristol Parkway.

/from

For example, trntxt.uk/bathspa will show the full departure board for Bath Spa.

/from/to

For example, trntxt.uk/paddington/reading will find trains departing Paddington, calling at Reading. You can then swap the journey's direction with the link at the top of the page.

This site is designed to work on any mobile device. There are no images or any other fancy things, so you get the data you need as quickly as possible.

Protip: pin journeys to your smartphone's homescreen for one-touch access to your favourite routes.
